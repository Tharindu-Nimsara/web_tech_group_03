Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the University of Sri Jayewardenepura Student Project Showcase collects, uses, and protects your information.

Transparency

We are transparent about what data we collect and how we use it.

Security

Your data is protected with industry-standard security measures.

Data Control

You have control over your personal information and project data.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name, student ID, and university email address
  • Department, academic year, and program information
  • Contact information for project collaboration
  • Profile information and academic credentials

Project Information

  • Project titles, descriptions, and documentation
  • Images, videos, and other media files
  • Source code and technical specifications
  • Collaboration and team member information

Usage Information

  • Website interaction data and navigation patterns
  • Search queries and project viewing history
  • Device information and browser specifications
  • IP addresses and geographic location data

How We Use Your Information

Platform Operations

  • Facilitate project submissions and reviews
  • Enable user authentication and account management
  • Provide search and discovery functionality
  • Generate analytics and usage statistics

Academic Purposes

  • Showcase student achievements and innovations
  • Support academic research and collaboration
  • Facilitate knowledge sharing within the university
  • Maintain academic records and portfolios

Communication

  • Send project status updates and notifications
  • Provide technical support and assistance
  • Share platform updates and new features
  • Facilitate communication between users

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

Within the University

  • Faculty and staff for academic evaluation purposes
  • IT department for technical support and maintenance
  • Administration for compliance and record-keeping

Public Information

  • Published projects are publicly viewable on the platform
  • Student names and project details in published works
  • Aggregated statistics and anonymized data

Legal Requirements

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect the rights and safety of users
  • To prevent fraud or security breaches

Data Security

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Backup and disaster recovery procedures
  • Staff training on data protection practices

Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Export your project data in a standard format
  • Objection: Object to certain uses of your information

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Specifically:

  • Account information: Until account deletion or graduation + 2 years
  • Project data: Permanently, unless specifically requested for deletion
  • Usage logs: 12 months for analytics and security purposes
  • Communication records: 3 years for support and legal compliance
