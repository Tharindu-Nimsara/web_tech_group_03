Project Submission Guidelines

Please read these guidelines carefully before submitting your project to ensure quality and compliance.

Submission Process

Follow our structured submission process for project approval and publication.

Quality Standards

Maintain academic integrity and technical excellence in all submissions.

Community Guidelines

Respect fellow students and contribute positively to our academic community.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Must be a current student or recent graduate of the University of Sri Jayewardenepura
  • Project must be academic in nature (coursework, research, thesis, or independent study)
  • All team members must be identified and credited appropriately
  • Projects must have been completed within the last 3 years
  • Supervisor or faculty advisor approval required for all submissions

Content Standards

Required Information

  • Clear project title and comprehensive description
  • Complete list of contributors with their roles
  • Department and academic year information
  • Supervisor/advisor details and contact information
  • Project timeline and completion date
  • Technologies, methodologies, or tools used

Technical Requirements

  • High-quality images or screenshots (minimum 1200x800 pixels)
  • Demo videos should be under 5 minutes and in MP4 format
  • Documentation files in PDF format (maximum 10MB)
  • Source code repositories must be publicly accessible
  • All external links must be functional and relevant

Prohibited Content

  • Plagiarized or non-original work
  • Content that violates intellectual property rights
  • Inappropriate, offensive, or discriminatory material
  • Commercial advertisements or promotional content
  • Personal information of individuals without consent

Review Process

Review Timeline: 5-7 business days

  1. Initial Screening: Automated checks for completeness and format compliance
  2. Academic Review: Faculty evaluation of content quality and academic merit
  3. Technical Review: Assessment of technical documentation and functionality
  4. Final Approval: Administrative approval and publication scheduling

You will receive email notifications at each stage of the review process. If revisions are needed, detailed feedback will be provided to help you improve your submission.

Post-Publication Guidelines

  • Respond promptly to comments and questions from the community
  • Update project information if significant changes occur
  • Report any issues or concerns to the administration team
  • Maintain professional communication in all interactions
  • Credit collaborators and acknowledge support appropriately

Contact Information

For submission support: projects@sjp.ac.lk

For technical issues: support@sjp.ac.lk

For general inquiries: info@sjp.ac.lk
